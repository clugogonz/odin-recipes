Fetuccine Alfredo

About this dish

Thick, tender fettuccine noodles are bathed in a rich, golden sauce of melted butter and savory parmesan, often finished with a hint of cracked black pepper for a subtle bite. It’s the ultimate comfort food—a lush, creamy symphony of salt and fat that clings perfectly to every strand of pasta for a melt-in-your-mouth experience.

Ingredients

  • 24 oz dry fetuccine pasta
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 pint heavy cream
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 dash garlic salt
  • 3/4 cup grated Romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccine pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Melt butter into cream in a large saucepan over low heat; add salt, pepper, and garlic salt.
  3. Increase the heat to medium; stir in grated Romano and Parmesan cheese until melted and sauce has thickened.
  4. Add cooked pasta to sauce and toss until thoroughly coated; serve immediately.
