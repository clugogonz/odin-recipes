Beef Tacos

About this dish

Hard-browned ground beef seasoned with a heavy hand of cumin, chili powder, and garlic, loaded into warm, pliable flour tortillas. Itâ€™s a straightforward, salty, and savory build topped with a sharp squeeze of lime and some fresh onion to cut through the fat.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive olive
  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 8 corn or flour tortillas
  • Finely chopped lettuce
  • Shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • Choppped tomatoes
  • Chopped red onions

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in skillet over medium high heat. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes. Drain any fat.
  2. Add the chili powder, cumin, dried oregano, garlic powder, salt, pepper, tomato paste and water. Stir to combine and continue cooking over medium-low heat until the sauce has thickened, about 3-5 minutes
  3. Serve warm over tortillas with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese and red onions, or your other desired toppings.
